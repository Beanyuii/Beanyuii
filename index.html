ABOUT OUR BAKERY

We Made For You With Love

Life is uncertain, but there's one thing you can always count on – the deliciousness of freshly baked pastries. At our bakery, we strive to make each treat a small moment of happiness that you can savor with each bite. From flaky croissants to decadent cakes, every one of our creations is made with love and the freshest ingredients. Come indulge in the sweetness of life with us at our bakery.

"Use products from nature for what it's worth - but never too early, nor too late. Fresh is the new sweet."

Head Baker and Owner: Yeon Park
